What We Do

Play Therapy

Watch your child blossom through play! Our play therapy sessions allow children to explore their emotions and experiences at their own pace, helping them communicate, resolve trauma, and grow.

Occupational Therapy

Our occupational therapy program is designed to help individuals master essential motor skills for daily activities. Through customized programs, we enhance coordination, balance, and self-control, paving the way for independence.

Picture Exchange Communication System (PECS)

Imagine your child or young adult communicating their needs and thoughts effortlessly! PECS empowers those with limited communication abilities to express themselves using pictures, seamlessly integrating at home, school, or in the community.

Speech Therapy

Unlock the power of speech! Our speech therapy services help individuals who have difficulty speaking or swallowing. We focus on improving their ability to use their voice and muscles correctly, enhancing their communication skills and confidence.

Behavioral Therapy

Transform behavior, transform life! Our behavioral therapy focuses on reinforcing positive behaviors and reducing negative ones. This targeted approach helps individuals learn new, healthier behaviors, leading to significant improvements.

Music, Sports, and Communication Classes

Boost social skills, physical coordination, and expressive abilities through our specialized classes in music, sports, and communication. These fun and engaging activities contribute to overall development and well-being.

Empowerment Pathways: Tailored Training Programs for Teen Skill Mastery

  • Service Excellence: Develop enhanced skills relevant to the service industry, preparing for potential roles.
  • Financial Wizardry: Learn makeup application, graphic design, musical instrument playing, and vocal improvement.
  • Artistic Mastery: Foster artistic skills through graphic design, music, singing, and more.
  • Fitness Fusion: Improve physical fitness, sports-specific skills, and prepare for competitions.

Our Facilities

School Bus

We provide safe and reliable transportation for all our students, ensuring they arrive at the center and back home on time.

Playground

Our indoor and outdoor play areas are designed to be fun and safe, encouraging physical activity and social interaction.

Healthy Canteen

Our canteen provides nutritious meals and snacks, catering to the dietary needs of all our students.

Positive Learning

Our day care service offers a secure and engaging environment for children to learn and play while receiving the support they need.
